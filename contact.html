❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Our average delivery time is 30–45 minutes depending on your location within our service area in Cavite.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Cash on Delivery (COD). GCash and card payments are coming soon!

What is your delivery coverage area?

We deliver within Imus, Bacoor, Dasmariñas, General Trias, and surrounding areas in Cavite.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it by calling us directly at (046) 123-4567.

Is there a minimum order amount?

There is no minimum order amount! However, orders above ₱500 qualify for free delivery.

Do you have a loyalty or rewards program?

We're currently building our MCCAT Rewards program. Sign up for our newsletter to be the first to know!
