📖 Our Story

Born From a Love of Great Food

MCCAT started in 2020 as a small family kitchen in Imus, Cavite. What began as a weekend passion project — serving burgers and fried chicken to neighbors — quickly grew into something bigger.

Our founder, inspired by the simple joy that a great burger brings, wanted to create a brand that felt personal, fast, and genuinely delicious. The name "MCCAT" represents the playful spirit of our brand — bold, friendly, and a little bit cheeky.

Today, we serve thousands of happy customers across Cavite every month. Every patty is hand-pressed, every sauce is house-made, and every order is prepared with the same love as that very first weekend kitchen.

🎯 What Drives Us

Mission, Vision & Promise

🎯

Our Mission

To make quality, delicious food accessible and affordable for every Filipino family. We believe great food shouldn't break the bank, and that every bite should feel special.

🔭

Our Vision

To become the most loved and trusted online food brand in the Philippines — delivering happiness, one order at a time, in every barangay, city, and province.

🤝

Our Promise

Every order from MCCAT comes with a promise: fresh ingredients, honest pricing, and delivery that's always on time. Your satisfaction is our only metric of success.
